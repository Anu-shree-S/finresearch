Agentic Research Lab

Focused tools for evaluating research novelty, comparing conflicting evidence, generating feasible methodologies and formatting citations.

Novelty Checker Contradiction Detector Idea-to-Methodology Citation Generator

N Novelty Checker

What this does: Performs a holistic novelty analysis across the selected papers. You may optionally enter a proposed idea; when left blank, the agent maps established themes, emerging combinations, overlaps and promising novelty opportunities directly from the literature.

! Contradiction Detector

What this does: Finds conflicting findings and explains whether the disagreement is genuine or caused by different datasets, metrics or methods.

M Idea-to-Methodology

What this does: Builds an evidence-grounded research design. You may enter an idea, or leave the field blank for the agent to derive a feasible project direction from the strongest gap in the selected literature.

C Paper Citation Generator

What this does: Reads the bibliographic details from each selected paper and formats the citation in commonly used academic styles. Always verify the generated details against the paper's first page or publisher record.

⇩ Export Research Package

What this does: Combines the generated research outputs and selected document names into a professionally formatted report.

PDF:Best for submitting, printing or sharing a fixed-layout report.
Word:Best when you need to edit the report further in Microsoft Word.
